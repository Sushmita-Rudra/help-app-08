Health Emergency Lifeline Program

We are ....

The local Health Emergency Lifeline Program, Inc. (H.E.L.P.) is a Lifeline Program Partner with Philips Lifeline.

HELP buys and then leases equipment to subscribers within Nodaway County to use to signal when a medical emergency occurs.

Knowing that emergency assistance is available with the push of a button allows subscribers to remain in their own home and maintain an independent lifestyle.


Vision statement

HELP volunteers are dedicated in helping their neighbors in need.

We aim at providing dependable and reliable services linking subscribers to an emergency response center in case of a health emergency.

To use appropriate technology at resonable cost, therby allowing the subscriber to remain independently in the comfort and safety of their personal residence..


Our programs

As an all-volunteer organization, Lifeline services are provided by Nodaway County community members who find fulfillment in serving their neighbors in need. Some of our famous program featuresinclude

    *Caring Community Volunteers Provide Program Services

    *Emergency Response at the Touch of a Button

    *In-Home Emergency Assistance Provided by Responders Chosen by the Subscriber

    *policy of Non-Discrimination
